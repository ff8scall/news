## Strategic Deep-Dive

Nvidia's strategic focus on modular AI infrastructure is fundamentally altering the electronics manufacturing landscape, compelling component manufacturers to ascend the value chain. This shift is most evident in the performance and strategic direction of Delta Electronics, which has emerged as a key player in modular production and comprehensive system integration. By moving beyond individual components to provide integrated power and cooling modules, Delta is capturing a larger share of the AI infrastructure market.

This evolution is also triggering a ripple effect across its supply chain, driving aggressive capacity investments from partners like rack manufacturer JPP Holding and battery module specialist Dynapack Inter. As AI data centers move toward standardized, modular builds to speed up deployment, the role of system integrators like Delta becomes indispensable. These integrators manage the complex interplay between high-density power delivery and thermal stability, ensuring that Nvidia’s hardware operates at peak efficiency.

This transition from component-level manufacturing to high-value system-level solutions is securing higher margins and tighter long-term partnerships within the global AI hardware ecosystem.
