## Strategic Deep-Dive

### Anthropic's 'Mythos' Model: Technical Impact and Shifting Threat Landscape

The emergence of Anthropic's latest AI model, 'Mythos,' represents another expansion of the performance limits of generative AI, while simultaneously posing a new national security threat. Mythos' advanced code generation capabilities and complex logical reasoning functions exhibit "dual-use" characteristics, meaning they can be exploited by cyber attackers to develop more sophisticated malware or explore vulnerabilities in critical infrastructure networks. In particular, national critical infrastructure such as power grids, water treatment facilities, and communication networks are vulnerable to attacks powered by such high-performance AI, making the advancement of technical defenses a matter of urgency.

### Japanese Government's Strategic Response System for Critical Infrastructure Protection

In response to the proliferation of Mythos, the Japanese government is establishing a multifaceted strategy to strengthen the cybersecurity of its national critical infrastructure. This is a comprehensive approach encompassing not only the introduction of technology but also legislative and institutional reforms.

1.  **Complete Reconstruction of Cybersecurity Infrastructure:** Moving away from traditional firewall-centric security, a 'Zero Trust' architecture is being implemented in critical infrastructure. AI-powered real-time anomaly detection systems (IDS) are being enhanced to establish a technical defense line that can immediately identify and block sophisticated attack patterns generated by Mythos-level AI.
2.  **Reinforcement of Legislative and Regulatory Framework:** Security guidelines that key critical infrastructure operators must adhere to are being elevated to legally binding requirements, and international cooperation to prevent the misuse of AI technology is being spearheaded. This is regarded as a proactive legislative measure to fill security gaps arising from technological advancements.
3.  **Public-Private Joint Crisis Response Governance:** A unified response system combining the government's security capabilities with the technical expertise of private AI specialists is being activated. This strengthens a cooperative system in which anticipated threat scenarios are simulated and defense strategies are shared each time a new AI model is released.

### Securing Technological Sovereignty and Security Resilience

This security enhancement measure aims not only to block external threats but also to safeguard Japan's technological sovereignty in the AI era and secure the resilience of the national system. With the emergence of Mythos as a catalyst, Japan is resetting cybersecurity as a core pillar of national security and plans to create a sustainable infrastructure operating environment capable of confronting intelligent threats.
