## Strategic Deep-Dive

### NEC's High-Capacity Optical Fiber Technology Innovation

Japanese technology giant NEC is strengthening its position in the global submarine cable market by highlighting its next-generation high-capacity optical fiber technology. To accommodate the exponentially increasing intercontinental data traffic, NEC is overcoming the limitations of physical infrastructure by introducing new technologies that maximize data transmission efficiency per unit area.

### Securing Technological Superiority in Submarine Communication Infrastructure

NEC's high-capacity optical fiber solution is characterized by minimizing signal interference and significantly increasing transmission distance compared to existing submarine cables. This is a core technology that meets the requirements of ultra-low latency and bandwidth scalability demanded by large-scale cloud service providers (CSPs) and global telecommunications companies. In particular, by securing the stability of Layer 1 infrastructure, it contributes to increasing the reliability of the global data highway.

### Increasing Global Demand and Strategic Partnerships

Submarine cables have become strategic assets that determine national competitiveness due to the digital transformation and the explosive growth in AI computing demand. NEC is expanding its collaboration with global hyperscalers based on its optical technology capabilities and is establishing itself as a key partner in building the next-generation global communication infrastructure through its high-performance optical cable solutions.
