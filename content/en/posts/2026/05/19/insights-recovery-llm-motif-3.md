# Motif Launches Clarity, a Next-Generation Financial Intelligence System

Zug, Switzerland – Motif, a Zug-based innovator in AI-driven asset management, has officially launched "Clarity," its next-generation financial intelligence system. While existing AI applications in the financial sector primarily rely on Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), connecting large language models (LLMs) to simple data feeds to retrieve information, Clarity aims for cognitive intelligence, independently "understanding" the causal relationships and risks within financial data. In high-stakes financial environments where real assets are involved, accurate contextual understanding is crucial, surpassing mere information delivery.

Backed by the strong capital of Temasek, Motif is striving to transform the global wealth management market paradigm by building a system that provides insights at the level of a professional consultant, going beyond simple chatbots.
