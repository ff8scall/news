## Strategic Deep-Dive

### Hitachi Partners with Anthropic to Revolutionize Industry with AI

Japanese industrial giant Hitachi has formed a strategic partnership with AI startup Anthropic, with plans to deploy the 'Claude' AI model to its approximately 290,000 employees worldwide. This large-scale adoption is a key strategy to enhance Hitachi's next-generation digital business platform, 'Lumada 3.0'.

### Enhancing Operational Efficiency with AI

Hitachi aims to leverage generative AI in complex industrial settings, such as manufacturing, energy, and infrastructure, to maximize internal process efficiency and develop high-level, customer-centric solutions. This move is expected to set a global benchmark for digital transformation (DX) as a traditional global manufacturing conglomerate integrates AI as the 'digital brain' across its entire organization.

### A New Era in Industrial Digitalization

By embracing AI technology, Hitachi is poised to revolutionize its operations and solidify its position as a leader in the industry. The partnership with Anthropic and the deployment of the 'Claude' AI model mark a significant step towards achieving this goal, and the outcomes are likely to have far-reaching implications for the future of industrial digitalization.
