## Strategic Deep-Dive

In response to the dominant growth of BYD, Japan's automotive sector is drafting a 'new road map' designed to protect its global market position. This strategic shift involves accelerating electric vehicle (EV) development and overhauling traditional mechanical engineering processes to embrace Software-Defined Vehicle (SDV) architectures. The roadmap serves as a blueprint for technological realignment, urging Japanese firms to prioritize digital integration and EV-centric innovation to counter the rising threat from highly integrated Chinese supply chains.
