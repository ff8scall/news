## Strategic Deep-Dive

## Challenging Proprietary Interconnects

Astera Labs is challenging the current AI hardware landscape with its new high-speed connectivity switch. The product is specifically designed to offer high-performance data transfers without the 'NVLink baggage' that often limits hardware interoperability. By providing a high-speed alternative, Astera aims to reduce the overhead and complexity associated with proprietary networking stacks in AI data centers.

## Toward Modular Data Center Design

This development is significant for the industry as it moves toward more modular and flexible AI infrastructure. As companies look to scale their compute clusters, the ability to achieve high-speed connectivity across diverse hardware components without vendor lock-in becomes a critical strategic advantage. Astera’s switch represents a major step toward standardizing high-performance interconnectivity in the AI era.
