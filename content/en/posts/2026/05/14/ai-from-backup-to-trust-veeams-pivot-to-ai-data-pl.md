## Strategic Deep-Dive

At VeeamON 2026 in New York, Veeam Software Group signaled a definitive architectural pivot, rebranding itself from a backup specialist to a foundational 'Data and AI Trust Platform.' CEO Anand Eswaran and President of Products Rehan Jalil articulated a vision where data availability is no longer just about disaster recovery, but about fueling the 'agentic era.' The company introduced a new architectural layer designed to guarantee data integrity for autonomous AI agents. This platform approach addresses a critical gap in the market: the need for a 'trust layer' that ensures the data fed into LLMs and RAG systems is sanitized, compliant, and highly available, effectively transforming backup repositories into active repositories of trusted corporate intelligence.
