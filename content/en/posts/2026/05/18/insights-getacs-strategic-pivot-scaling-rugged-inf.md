## Key Analysis

The "militarization" of commercial hardware is accelerating. The demand for drones now accounting for up to 10% of sales signifies that rugged PCs have transitioned beyond simple durable consumer goods, becoming integral components of tactical operations. This suggests that future hardware specification decisions may become subordinate to the trends in national defense budget allocations.
