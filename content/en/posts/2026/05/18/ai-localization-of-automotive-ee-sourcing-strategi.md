## Key Analysis

The automotive industry is transitioning from a manufacturing-centric model to an AI platform-centric one, thereby assigning new responsibilities to hardware supply chains. These responsibilities now extend beyond simple parts provision to include guaranteeing robust cybersecurity and sufficient AI computational capabilities. The year 2027 is poised to be a critical turning point where Taiwanese electronics companies will potentially seize leadership in the automotive industry.
