## Key Analysis

The "de-Sinicization" of the drone supply chain is evolving beyond a mere trend, becoming a critical hardware strategy intertwined with national security. Taiwan's enhanced collaboration with AUVSI (Association for Unmanned Vehicle Systems International) is assessed as a strategic move to secure a unique position in the global unmanned systems market, connecting its hardware manufacturing capabilities with geopolitical reliability.
