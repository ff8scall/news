## Strategic Deep-Dive

The Indian tech services landscape is navigating an 'AI Deflation' crisis, where AI-driven productivity gains are devaluing traditional revenue models. As tasks that previously required extensive manual labor are automated, firms are facing a necessary strategic shift from legacy man-hour billing toward outcome-based pricing. While headcounts currently remain stable, this structural friction indicates a transitional phase before an inevitable AI-induced labor disruption occurs across the sector.
