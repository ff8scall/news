## Strategic Deep-Dive

London-based Primer has secured a $100M (€86.2M) Series C round to catalyze the expansion of its 'Autonomous AI Payments' platform into the US market. The strategic roadmap involves a transition from traditional rule-based orchestration to sophisticated AI-driven decisioning, aimed at capturing over one-third of global revenue from the US by 2028. This move signifies a broader trend of fintech platforms evolving into intelligent, self-optimizing financial ecosystems.
