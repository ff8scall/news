## Strategic Deep-Dive

The collaboration showcased at the Dimensity Developer Conference (MDDC 2026) represents a major technical feat. By integrating Phison’s aiDAPTIV technology with MediaTek’s Dimensity 9500 platform, the teams managed to run a 20-billion parameter LLM on a single edge device.

This breakthrough leverages advanced memory management to expand the capabilities of DRAM and NAND, allowing large models to be processed locally. This eliminates the need for constant cloud connectivity, significantly enhancing privacy and reducing inference latency for end-users.
