## Strategic Deep-Dive

- Revenue vs. Profitability Gap: Rising AI server prices have led to record revenues for leading ODMs such as Foxconn and Quanta. However, the extreme cost of components like high-end GPUs has squeezed gross margins, creating a disconnect between top-line growth and bottom-line health.
- The Consignment Shift: To mitigate the financial burden of purchasing expensive components and carrying inventory, manufacturers are negotiating with customers to shift from the traditional 'buy-sell' model to a 'consignment' model.
- Strategic Balancing: ODMs are increasingly focusing on ASIC-based solutions and general-purpose servers. These segments often offer better margin profiles compared to the high-volume but high-cost standard AI server assemblies.
