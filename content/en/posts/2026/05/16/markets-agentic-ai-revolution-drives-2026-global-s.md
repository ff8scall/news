## Strategic Deep-Dive

The global server market is poised for a significant transformation in 2026, with shipments expected to grow by 19.2% year-on-year. This surge is primarily attributed to the rise of 'Agentic AI'—autonomous AI entities that require continuous and high-intensity computing resources. As these AI agents become integrated into enterprise and consumer applications, the demand for robust server infrastructure is pushing the total shipment volume toward the historic 20 million unit milestone.

Unlike previous AI iterations, Agentic AI necessitates a more balanced approach between GPU acceleration and CPU-driven logic processing, forcing a fundamental redesign of server architectures to handle persistent, autonomous workloads.
