## Strategic Deep-Dive

## Capital Reallocation and AI Momentum

TSMC’s decision to reduce its stake in Vanguard International Semiconductor (VIS) marks the second significant portfolio adjustment within a two-week window. These transactions underscore a deliberate strategy to reallocate capital toward AI-driven opportunities.

## Shifting Away from Legacy Holdings

While the relationship between TSMC and VIS has been foundational, the current market landscape demands immense liquidity for AI infrastructure. By divesting from legacy foundry-focused holdings, TSMC is positioning itself to fund the high-stakes expansion required for the AI era, prioritizing advanced manufacturing capabilities over mature process investments.
