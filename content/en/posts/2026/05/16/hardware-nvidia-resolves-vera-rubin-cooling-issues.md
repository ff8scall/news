## Strategic Deep-Dive

Market anxieties regarding Nvidia’s next-generation Vera Rubin platform have subsided following reports that design issues tied to cooling architecture changes have been resolved. The transition to HBM4 and higher thermal requirements necessitated a robust architectural overhaul. Nvidia has now solidified its mass-production roadmap in coordination with key ODMs and suppliers.

This confirmation puts the development cycle back on track, with the supply chain preparing for a significant 3Q26 volume ramp, ensuring Nvidia maintains its momentum in the high-end AI silicon market.
