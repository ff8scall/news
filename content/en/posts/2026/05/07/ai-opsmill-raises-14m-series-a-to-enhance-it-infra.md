## Strategic Deep-Dive

OpsMill has closed a $14 million Series A funding round led by IRIS, with participation from BGV and existing investors Serena and Partech. The company’s Infrahub platform addresses a critical bottleneck in the automation era: the reliability of infrastructure data. As organizations pivot toward AI-driven agentic operations, the platform ensures that the underlying data infrastructure is accurate and action-ready.

Its efficacy is highlighted by production deployments at TikTok and a major European cloud provider, where it reportedly slashed deployment times from five days to just fifteen minutes, effectively decoupling service delivery from manual overhead.
