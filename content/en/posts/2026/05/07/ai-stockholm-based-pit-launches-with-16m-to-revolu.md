## Strategic Deep-Dive

Pit, a Stockholm-based platform, has emerged from stealth with $16 million in funding led by a16z and Lakestar. The round saw participation from a high-pedigree group of strategic angels from OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Deel, and Revolut. Pit’s core value proposition is 'AI-native software for enterprise operations,' allowing businesses to build and deploy custom operational tools at unprecedented speeds.

Early metrics from enterprise clients like Voi and Stena Recycling demonstrate a dramatic acceleration in deployment cycles, moving from a standard of weeks down to mere days.
