This incident serves as a clear example of Anthropic exercising significant governance control over its ecosystem. When a core commercial metric (pricing) changes, the rights and access of affiliated developers can be immediately impacted. This raises questions for the industry regarding:
*   **Contractual Clarity:** The necessity for explicit, detailed agreements outlining how pricing changes affect third-party developers.

### 4.3. Operational Risk Management
