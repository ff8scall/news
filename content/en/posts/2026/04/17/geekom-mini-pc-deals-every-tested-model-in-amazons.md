## Executive Summary
Geekom's mini PC lineup is seeing significant price cuts during Amazon's Spring Sale. This report evaluates the performance density of various models, focusing on thermal management and the integration of high-performance mobile processors in ultra-compact chassis.

## Strategic Deep-Dive
Geekom has established a reputation for packing high-performance hardware into sub-1-liter chassis. During the Amazon Spring Sale, models featuring the latest Intel Meteor Lake and AMD Phoenix architectures are highly sought after. The technical challenge in these devices is "Thermal Throttling"—how the system maintains high clock speeds without overheating.

Geekom addresses this with customized vapor chambers and high-airflow fans. For users, these mini PCs represent a move toward "Invisible Computing," where the desktop disappears behind the monitor without sacrificing the multi-core performance required for video editing or software development.
