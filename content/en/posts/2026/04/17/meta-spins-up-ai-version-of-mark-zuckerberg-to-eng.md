## Executive Summary
Meta has launched an AI-driven digital twin of CEO Mark Zuckerberg to interact with its global workforce. This initiative utilizes advanced synthetic voice and visual rendering to scale internal engagement and provide real-time responses to employee inquiries.

## Strategic Deep-Dive
Meta’s deployment of an AI version of Mark Zuckerberg is a high-stakes experiment in corporate digital twin integration. Technically, the AI utilizes Meta's Llama-3 architecture, fine-tuned on Zuckerberg’s public transcripts, internal memos, and video data to capture his idiosyncratic speech patterns and strategic philosophy. The avatar employs real-time generative lip-syncing and facial animation to create a convincing presence during VR or video-based interactions.

While intended to bridge the gap between leadership and a massive global workforce, it raises technical and ethical questions regarding the authenticity of leadership and the potential for "AI-mediated management" where algorithms replace human empathy.
