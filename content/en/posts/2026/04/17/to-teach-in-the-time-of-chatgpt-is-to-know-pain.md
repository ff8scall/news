## Executive Summary
Educators are experiencing a profound identity crisis and workload increase due to the integration of generative AI in classrooms. The challenge of distinguishing between AI-generated and human-authored work is forcing a radical redesign of pedagogical standards.

## Strategic Deep-Dive
The integration of generative AI into high education has disrupted traditional methods of evaluating student comprehension. Educators now face the "Detection Dilemma," where AI-writing detectors consistently produce false positives and negatives, making them unreliable for disciplinary action. Technically, this necessitates a shift toward oral examinations, in-class supervised writing, and "Process-based Grading"—evaluating the steps of an assignment rather than just the final output.

This pedagogical disruption is painful because it requires a fundamental overhaul of curricula that have remained static for decades.
