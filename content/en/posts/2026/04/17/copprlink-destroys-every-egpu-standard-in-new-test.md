## Executive Summary
The new CopprLink interface has set a record for eGPU performance, allowing an RTX 5090 to function at 98% efficiency compared to internal installation. However, the requirement for $2,300 in specialized hardware poses a significant barrier to mainstream adoption.

## Strategic Deep-Dive
CopprLink represents the first true bypass of the bandwidth bottlenecks that have plagued external GPUs for a decade. While Thunderbolt 4/5 provides a limited 40-80Gbps, CopprLink utilizes a direct PCIe 5.0 x16 link over a proprietary copper-fiber hybrid cable, achieving massive throughput that matches internal motherboard slots. In technical benchmarks, an RTX 5090 connected via CopprLink showed less than 2% performance loss in 4K ray-tracing workloads.

The drawback is the $2,300 cost for the host interface card and the specialized enclosure, effectively pricing this solution as a niche product for extreme enthusiasts and professional AI developers.
