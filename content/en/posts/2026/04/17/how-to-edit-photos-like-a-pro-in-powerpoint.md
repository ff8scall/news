## Executive Summary
PowerPoint's evolving feature set now includes advanced image manipulation tools previously reserved for professional editing software. Understanding these built-in capabilities allows users to streamline workflow without relying on external design applications.

## Strategic Deep-Dive
The evolution of Microsoft PowerPoint into a pseudo-image editor is a result of years of incremental updates to its graphics engine. By leveraging the same underlying libraries used in other Microsoft Office 365 products, PowerPoint now offers non-destructive photo editing, including sophisticated background removal and layer management. This technical shift democratizes design capabilities, allowing non-designers to perform complex visual tasks through a familiar interface.

The integration of AI-powered "Design Ideas" further automates composition, making professional aesthetics accessible to the average user.
