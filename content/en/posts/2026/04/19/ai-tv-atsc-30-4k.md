## Strategic Deep-Dive
This article highlights the resurgence of Over-the-Air (OTA) television as a cost-effective alternative to cable. It focuses on the transition to NextGen TV (ATSC 3.0), which delivers 4K and HDR content for free. The ClearStream 2Max is recommended as the top antenna, though the report notes it lacks an included coaxial cable—a vital detail for buyers.

Other models like the Winegard FlatWave Amped are discussed for their 50-mile range. Technical insights include the use of tools like King SL1000 for signal measurement, legal protections under FCC OTARD rules for outdoor installations, and DVR solutions like AirTV and HDHomeRun for recording local broadcasts.
