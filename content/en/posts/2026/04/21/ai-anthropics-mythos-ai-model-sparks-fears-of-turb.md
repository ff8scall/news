## Executive Summary
- Anthropic's restricted Mythos model is causing concern among cybersecurity experts for its ability to automate advanced hacking techniques. The speed at which it can identify zero-day vulnerabilities may outpace the global ability to patch them.

## Strategic Deep-Dive

The debut of Anthropic's Mythos model has sent shockwaves through the cybersecurity community. While designed for advanced reasoning, its proficiency in code analysis makes it a formidable weapon for "turbocharged" hacking. Mythos can ingest vast codebases to identify obscure zero-day vulnerabilities in seconds—a process that typically takes human red teams weeks or months.

The fear is not just the discovery, but the automated generation of polymorphic exploits that can bypass traditional antivirus signatures. This acceleration of the offense-defense cycle threatens to leave critical infrastructure exposed, as software vendors struggle to develop and deploy patches at "AI speed."
