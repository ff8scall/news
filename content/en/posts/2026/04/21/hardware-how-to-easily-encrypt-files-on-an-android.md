## Executive Summary
- This tutorial outlines the process of using OpenKeychain to encrypt sensitive files on Android devices using PGP standards. It emphasizes the importance of local encryption for secure data sharing in an age of frequent leaks.

## Strategic Deep-Dive

In an era where digital privacy is constantly under siege, local file encryption has transitioned from a niche requirement to a daily necessity. This guide recommends OpenKeychain, a free, open-source Android application that implements the OpenPGP standard. By creating a public-private key pair directly on your smartphone, you can ensure that documents, photos, and sensitive data remain unreadable to anyone but the intended recipient.

The article breaks down the process of importing keys, encrypting attachments for email, and managing digital signatures, providing Android users with military-grade security tools that fit in their pockets.
