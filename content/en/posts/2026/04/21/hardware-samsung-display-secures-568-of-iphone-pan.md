## Strategic Deep-Dive

Samsung Display (SDC) has reinforced its position as the undisputed king of premium OLED hardware. According to market research firm Omdia, SDC captured a commanding 56.8% share of the iPhone display supply chain in 2025, an 8 percentage point jump from the previous year. Total shipments reached 142 million units, reflecting a 15% year-over-year increase.

This dominance is intrinsically linked to SDC's early and aggressive investment in EUV-integrated panel manufacturing and LTPO (Low-Temperature Polycrystalline Oxide) backplane technology. While Chinese rival BOE has attempted to undercut SDC on price, it has failed to meet Apple’s stringent yield and color accuracy requirements for the Pro and Pro Max models. SDC’s ability to scale high-quality production of complex panel designs ensures that it remains the primary beneficiary of Apple’s hardware roadmap.
