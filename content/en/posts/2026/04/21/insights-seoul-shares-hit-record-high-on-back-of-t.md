## Strategic Deep-Dive

The South Korean stock market has reached an unprecedented milestone, with the KOSPI hitting record highs driven by a massive rally in tech shares. This surge is largely attributed to global demand for HBM (High Bandwidth Memory) and semiconductors, where South Korean giants like Samsung and SK Hynix dominate. For global investors, the Seoul market serves as a barometer for the electronics supply chain, signaling robust growth in the AI era.

This expansion reflects the strategic value of South Korea's hardware ecosystem.
