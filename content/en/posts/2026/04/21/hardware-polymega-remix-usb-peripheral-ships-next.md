## Executive Summary
- After years of production delays, Playmaji's $199 Polymega Remix USB peripheral will begin shipping next month. The device allows users to play and digitize physical retro CDs and cartridges on modern Windows 11 PCs and handhelds like the Steam Deck.

## Strategic Deep-Dive

Playmaji has announced that the Polymega Remix, a $199 USB peripheral designed for retro gaming preservation, is finally set to ship next month. This device bridges the massive gap between vintage physical media—such as original PlayStation CDs and SNES cartridges (via modular adapters)—and contemporary computing hardware. By connecting the Remix to a Windows 11 PC, laptop, or gaming handheld like the Steam Deck, users can bypass standard emulation complexities.

The system allows for high-fidelity playback and the ability to digitize physical collections into a modern, organized software library. Despite facing years of manufacturing setbacks, the Polymega Remix offers a compelling value proposition for enthusiasts who want to preserve the tangibility of their game collections while leveraging the performance of modern processors and displays.
