## Strategic Deep-Dive

The automotive market is seeing a convergence where mainstream manufacturers offer features previously reserved for luxury brands. This "premium comfort" trend allows consumers to enjoy high-quality materials and advanced ADAS without the "badge premium." By analyzing vehicle architecture and technology integration, it's clear that SUVs like the Kia Telluride rival traditional premium competitors in everything but name. This report explores why the luxury logo is becoming less relevant in the face of engineering parity.
