## Strategic Deep-Dive

Samsung continues to lead the Android ecosystem with its robust software update policy. As Android 17 approaches, Galaxy users are eager to know when they can expect the One UI 9 update. Typically, Samsung begins its beta program in late summer, followed by a stable rollout for flagship devices like the Galaxy S-series and the latest Foldables in the fourth quarter.

This consistent rollout strategy is part of Samsung's broader goal to provide up to seven years of security and OS updates for its premium lineup, ensuring hardware longevity.
